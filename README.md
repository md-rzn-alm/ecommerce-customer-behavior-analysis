🛒 Customer Behavior Analysis Dashboard

📌 Overview : 

This project analyzes customer shopping behavior to uncover key business insights related to revenue, customer segmentation, and purchasing patterns.
It helps businesses make data-driven decisions to improve sales, retention, and customer experience.

📊 Dashboard Preview : 

🎯 Objectives :

    Identify top revenue-driving product categories
    Analyze customer demographics and behavior
    Evaluate sales performance across segments
    Improve customer retention and satisfaction

🧰 Tech Stack : 
      
      Python (Pandas, Matplotlib)
      SQL (MySQL)
      Power BI
      Jupyter Notebook

📈 Key Metrics : 

    👥 Total Customers: 3.9K+
    💰 Avg Purchase Amount: $59.76
    ⭐ Avg Rating: 3.75 / 5

🔍 Key Insights : 

💰 Revenue Insights : 

       Clothing contributes ~50% of total revenue → primary business driver , 
       Accessories show strong secondary growth opportunity

👥 Customer Behavior : 

       73% customers are non-subscribers → retention opportunity , 
       Young adults are highest revenue-generating segment

📦 Sales Insights : 

       Clothing dominates both sales volume and revenue , 
       Outerwear underperforms → needs pricing/marketing optimization

⭐ Customer Experience : 

        Avg rating (3.75) indicates improvement opportunities

💡 Business Recommendations : 

      Introduce loyalty/subscription programs

      Focus marketing on high-performing categories

      Improve low-rated products

      Use bundling strategies to increase AOV
